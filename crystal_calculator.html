SAP Crystal Solutions License Calculator

UX design

It is difficult to navigate through different SAP Crystal related buying options. There are 6 variations that customer can get by download directly, request via email, or purchase on SAP Store (a e-commerce platform for customer to buy enterprise software online). Each product has different capabilities, license purchase options.


Currently there are no tool for customers to determine and compare the product that fits their needs, so usually it requires extra research time or help from real person.

Getting to the right product simpler and faster.
