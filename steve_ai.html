About Me

It is a semester-long project for Advanced Design for Artificial Intelligence Design course at UT Austin. Using user-centered design methodology, we came up with an AI technology mobile application that designed to solve several pain points throughout people’s career switch process.

Steve.ai is an AI-based conversational assistant that helps people switching to tech career.

More people want to switch their job, and many jobs in technology field don’t require a degree and value candidates’ experience and skills. However, career switch in this area is difficult to people.

The process can be challenging, stressful, and not everyone will end up switching their career successfully. AS a student focusing on information science and seeing many of my other friends also trying to change their career path to technology related field. We want to focus on this problem.

(before) How might we use AI technology to assist people during their career switch process?

We interviewed 5 people who is currently in the preparation process to switch their career in technology field, try to figure out their thoughts, challenges, and their experience about this process.
